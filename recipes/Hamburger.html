Hamburger Recipe

Description:

A hamburger is a classic sandwich built around a juicy ground beef patty, typically seasoned with salt and pepper and cooked on a grill or stovetop. The patty is placed inside a soft, lightly toasted bun and often topped with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomato slices, onions, and pickles. Condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise add extra flavor and moisture, creating a balanced combination of savory, tangy, and fresh tastes.

Simple yet satisfying, the hamburger is known for its versatility. It can be customized with different cheeses, sauces, or toppings like bacon and saut√©ed mushrooms. Whether served at a backyard cookout or a restaurant, the hamburger remains a popular comfort food that is hearty, flavorful, and easy to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook patties on a grill or skillet over medium-high heat until browned and cooked to your desired doneness.
  3. Add cheese on top during the last minute of cooking if desired.
  4. Toast the buns lightly.
  5. Assemble the burger with the patty and your preferred toppings and condiments.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy.
