Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy pasta dish known for its rich, velvety sauce and tender pieces of seasoned chicken. Grilled or pan-seared chicken breast is sliced and served over fettuccine noodles that are coated in a smooth Alfredo sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. The sauce clings to each strand of pasta, creating a comforting and indulgent flavor in every bite.

The dish is often finished with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan and chopped parsley for added flavor and color. Its combination of savory chicken, creamy sauce, and perfectly cooked pasta makes Chicken Alfredo a popular choice for both casual dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper, then cook in a pan over medium heat until fully cooked. Slice into strips.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and let it simmer for a few minutes. Stir in grated Parmesan until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add the cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly.
  6. Serve warm, topped with extra Parmesan and parsley if desired.
